We aims to build a project for Students title “Commerce and Connect ”Commerce platform where students can buy and sell their products, share course materials like PDF notes and lecture slides, as well as hire university's band singers and buy band show tickets. The platform will be developed to enable students to market their products and increase their earning potential while also providing an online marketplace for other students to purchase unique items. The platform will also allow students to hire university's band singers for events and shows and purchase tickets for upcoming band performances. In addition, the platform will provide a forum for students to share course materials, engage in academic discussions, and collaborate with their peers.
